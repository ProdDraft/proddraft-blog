## Content Track (60% of effort)

### Affiliate Posts — Weekly
- Format: Tool comparison or review, 1500-2500 words
- Target: High buyer intent keywords (e.g., "best AI coding tool 2026")
- Each post: real usage experience, affiliate links to every tool mentioned
- Affiliate programs: PartnerStack, Impact, direct tool affiliate pages

### Authority Posts — Monthly
- Format: Deep technical (paper breakdown, state-of-AI, product tutorials)
- Purpose: SEO authority, newsletter bait
- Example: "The state of LLMs — June 2026" (ongoing monthly series)

### Cadence
- 1 affiliate post Tuesday
- 1 authority post last week of month
- All posts cross-link to relevant Gumroad products and newsletter signup
